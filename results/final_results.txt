MedVisionX – Brain Tumor Classification Results

ResNet18 Baseline:
Accuracy: 83%

Vision Transformer:
Accuracy: 96%

SSL Vision Transformer:
Accuracy: 96%

Key Findings:

• Vision Transformers significantly outperform CNNs for MRI tumor classification.
• Transformer attention captures spatial tumor structure better than convolutional filters.
• Self-supervised learning improves feature representation but did not increase final accuracy due to dataset size.
• GradCAM confirms models focus on tumor regions.

Conclusion:

Transformer-based architectures are better suited for MRI tumor classification tasks than CNNs.
